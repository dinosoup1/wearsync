# Google Play Store Listing Copy

## App Title (max 30 chars)
WearSync – Wear OS App Finder

## Short Description (max 80 chars)
Discover which of your phone apps have a Wear OS watch version.

## Full Description (max 4000 chars)

Ever get a new Wear OS watch and wonder which of your phone apps have a watch companion? WearSync answers that question in seconds.

**What WearSync does:**
• Scans your installed phone apps
• Checks your watch for what's already there
• Searches the Play Store to find Wear OS versions you're missing
• Shows a clean list with one-tap links to install them on your watch

**No guesswork. No manual searching. Just a list of apps you can actually install.**

---

**Privacy-first by design**

WearSync collects zero user data:
• No accounts or sign-in required
• No analytics or telemetry
• No Firebase or crash reporting
• No data stored outside your device
• No data shared with anyone

The only network activity is checking the Play Store for Wear OS app availability and opening install links — nothing else leaves your device.

---

**How it works**

WearSync talks directly to your paired watch over Bluetooth using the Wearable Data Layer API. No internet required for the phone-watch communication. Results are cached after the first scan so subsequent launches are instant.

---

**Requirements**
• Android 8.0 or higher
• A paired Wear OS watch
• Both devices on the same Google account

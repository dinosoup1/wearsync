# Privacy Policy

**WearSync** | Last updated: March 2026

## Summary

WearSync collects no user data. Period.

## What We Collect

Nothing. WearSync does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage information.

## Network Activity

WearSync makes two types of network connections:

1. **Play Store lookups** — WearSync queries the Google Play Store to check whether your installed apps have Wear OS versions available. Only app package names (e.g. `com.spotify.music`) are used in these queries. No personal information is included.

2. **Install deep-links** — When you tap "Install" on an app, WearSync opens the Google Play Store listing for that app. WearSync does not make this network connection itself — the Play Store app handles it.

## Phone-Watch Communication

WearSync communicates with your paired Wear OS watch using Google's Wearable Data Layer API over Bluetooth or Wi-Fi Direct. This communication is local (between your phone and watch) and is handled entirely by Google Play Services. WearSync does not intercept, log, or transmit this data.

## Data Storage

WearSync stores one thing locally on your device: a cache of Play Store lookup results (which apps have Wear OS versions). This is stored in the app's private SharedPreferences on your device only. It is never transmitted anywhere and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

## Third-Party SDKs

WearSync contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or crash reporting SDKs. The only third-party dependency is Google Play Services (required for the Wearable Data Layer API).

## Children's Privacy

WearSync does not collect any data from any users, including children.
